SDG-4: Quality Education



explaination tut here!
SDG-4 focuses on ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.
Problems concentrating:
  • Target 4.1: Ensuring all girls and boys complete free, equitable, and quality primary and secondary education.
  • Target 4.3: Ensure equal access for all women and men to affordable and quality technical, vocational and tertiary education, including university.
  • Target 4.6: Lifelong learning


This free-educational website helps solving the former through:
  1. Free Access to Education: SDG 4 targets ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education, and a free website removes cost as a barrier. Anyone with an internet connection can access the knowledge and resources on your website.
  2. Increased Opportunities: Free educational websites can reach a wider audience, including people in remote areas or those who can't afford traditional education.
  3. Lifelong Learning: Websites can provide educational resources for all ages, promoting lifelong learning, another aspect of SDG 4.
  4. Content Focus: Aims on education which constitutes ages 9-13 years.
